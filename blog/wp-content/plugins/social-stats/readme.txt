== Description ==
** Track growth of your blog on social networks such as Facebook, Bitly, Google Buzz
* View most popular posts by days/weeks/months
* View graph of how your posts perform on different days 
* Real-time Analytics
* Track Top Content Creators
* Track Top Social Referrers
