A Contact Form to receive more calls, more subscribers and more contacts.

== Description ==
[ManyContacts](http://www.manycontacts.com/)  is a suite of Website Tools to turn your website into a sales machine: it captures leads from your website and converts visitors into calls, email subscribers and customers.

Over 10,000 Website use ManyContacts everyday to increase their conversion rate.

### Features: ###

*   Multiple type of forms: top bars ,popups & Full page when your visitor exit your website.
*   Multiple contact options: calls, see map, email subscription, contact form.
*   Data incrementing: manycontacts collects a lot of public information about your lead and tells you everything about them.
*   A/B testing

== Installation ==
1. Upload the `manycontacts-bar` folder and all its contents to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Paste your manycontacts bar snippet from [manycontacts.com](http://www.manycontacts.com/) into the text area and click save!
